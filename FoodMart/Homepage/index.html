Food Mart Dashboard

Project Overview

This project collected a demographic information of customer data as well as store, product, and promotion features. To discover customer segment distributions, We used SQL as database to store and query the data. Then Tableau public was used to visualize the results. Finally, we createa models to predict member card, customer occupation, cost and store sales, and calculated accuracy of predictions.
